October 4th, 2023

"I fell while running and got hurt, but I just brushed it off and got up, so I couldn't understand the child who hesitated and cried. I didn't get how my mom, shivering in the cold during the winter, felt because I rarely felt cold. I didn't understand my friend who expected empathy from me, even though I never talked about my own struggles. I didn't understand myself. Understanding was something too difficult for me, almost like a young child learning to walk."
